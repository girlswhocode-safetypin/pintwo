Resources

There are many volunteer opportunities that can help immigrants. You can volunteer in detention centers, school, and even clinics. If you are passionate in volunteer work and immigration this is for you.

Social Workers: You can help immigrants solve problems that go beyond legal dimensions in their daily lives. Social workers can help with coping with emotional distress. Mental health plays an important role in the support of asylum seekers. Use these resources to figure out how you can volunteer in a local detention center and help immigrants in need.

Translators: You can volunteer at local churches or at schools to teach english to immigrants. You can also assist in translation at citizenship interviews with the discretion of the court.
